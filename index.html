My Fish

I recently made the most amazing discovery!

It's going to turn the world of fish harvesting and care on its head. I just returned from a trip to the Caribbean where I took a day trip inland to see the ruins of the city of Tikal. As I was deep inside one of the ruins, I wandered down this hallway. At the end was this enormous engraving of a fish. Only it wasn't just a fish. It was a logoglyph surrounded by other ones. Somehow, I just could understanding the writing, even though I'd never seen it before. It described the Holy Fish Jutz'kar and his followers. The most holy of his followers were always a multiple of 3 inches in length. They cared for Jutz'kar and fed him. The next holy were his soldier fish - swordfish - and they were all multiples of 5 inches in length. Some were even as large as 250 inches!!

Based on this INCREDIBLE finding, I've decided to pay homage to the Great Fish Jutz'kar and his servants. As such, each fish in my collection has been designated with a badge based on their size and appropriate rank.
